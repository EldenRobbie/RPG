In order for this project to be considered completed, it must have the following features:

1. A working combat system that can call different monsters (individually) to fight.
    1. Attack
    2. Defend
    3. Item
    4. Flee

2. At least three NPC's to talk to who offer quests.

3. A working XP/Level system.

4. Two towns to visit with shops to buy items from.
