For this project I'm going to make a short, text_based RPG.

There will be a battle system where players can attack, defend, use items or flee.
(After getting these basics done, I want to eventually add skills with magic(MP) or skill points(SP) needed to use)
There will be at least five enemies to fight by the end of the short project.

There will be an experience/level system where stats will increase after leveling up.

There should be at least three different quests to go on.

There will be NPC's to talk to with dialogues choices.
